About Me

I have a degree in civil engineering and am currently pursuing a master’s in business administration and architectural engineering with a focus in construction management. Through my studies, I have become really interested in how the AEC industry is starting to shift with new innovations and technologies. A lot of what I have learned has been on the more traditional side, so seeing how artificial intelligence is beginning to impact design, decision-making, and construction processes has been especially interesting to me. Understanding how projects come together and how systems can be improved, not just designed is something I enjoy. This portfolio highlights my work throughout the semester, including AI news, class assignments, and examples of how AI can be applied in the AEC industry, along with how my perspective on it has developed over the course.
